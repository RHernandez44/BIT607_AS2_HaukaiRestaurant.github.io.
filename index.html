What We are About

Haukai Restaurant specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. The owners also want customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.
